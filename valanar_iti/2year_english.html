Safety & Best Practices

  • PPE & Electrical Safety
    Use gloves, goggles, ear protection; isolate supply and follow lockout-tagout (LOTO).
  • Refrigerant Recovery & Handling
    Safe recovery, cylinder handling, labeling and compliance with environmental rules.
  • Hot Work & Brazing Safety
    Use nitrogen purge, fire watch, goggles, and maintain clear workspace.
